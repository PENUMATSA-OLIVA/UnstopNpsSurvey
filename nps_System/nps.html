DevRev was founded by Dheeraj Pandey, former CEO of Nutanix and Manoj Agarwal, former executive at Nutanix.Our mission is to connect makers (Dev) to customers (Rev).
At the core of DevRev lies a knowledge graph that interconnects data across all departments, including support, sales, engineering, product, and customer success. With this data web as the foundation, the company brings the power of LLMs and Analytics to build copilots that connect everyone together to care about the customer. This new CRM, called OneCRM, streamlines real-time collaboration between departments and customers, and for the first time, integrates product development teams with customer relationships
